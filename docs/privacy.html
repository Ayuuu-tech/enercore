Privacy Policy

Enercore mobile application · Last updated: 14 July 2026

Enercore is operated by Enercore New Energy Private Limited ("Enercore", "we", "us"). The Enercore app is a solar-plant monitoring tool for our customers: it shows the live performance of the solar plants we operate for you, your monthly solar bills, and your support tickets. This policy explains what the app collects, why, and what you can do about it.

Who can use the app

Enercore is a business application for existing Enercore customers, their staff, and our vendors. Accounts are created for you by Enercore — the app is not open to the general public, and it is not directed at children.

What we collect

DataWhy we need it
Account details
Name, email address, and password (stored only as a cryptographic hash — never in plain text).		 To sign you in and to show you only the plants you are authorised to see.
Contact & business details (optional)
Phone number, company name, GST number, postal address.		 To raise your solar bills and to contact you about your plants. Provided by you or by Enercore.
Profile photo (optional) Shown in the app so colleagues recognise your account. Only if you upload one.
Support tickets
The messages you write.		 To respond to the issue you raised.
Plant telemetry
Generation, power, voltage and other readings from the solar equipment.		 This is the core of the service. It is data about equipment, not about you.

What we do not collect

  • We do not collect your device's location. The map in the app shows the fixed locations of solar plants, not you.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos (beyond a picture you deliberately upload), microphone, camera, call logs, or messages.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers, and we do not show ads.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not sell your personal data. Ever.

How your data is handled

  • All traffic between the app and our servers is encrypted over HTTPS.
  • Passwords are stored as salted hashes; nobody at Enercore can read your password.
  • Access is enforced on our servers, not just hidden in the app: you can only retrieve data for the plants assigned to your account.
  • Data is stored on Microsoft Azure and Supabase (PostgreSQL). Both act as our processors under contract.
  • Plant readings are fetched from the monitoring platforms of the equipment on your site (Trackso and IO.Next).

Who we share it with

Only the service providers above, and only so the app can function. We do not share your personal data with anyone else, except where the law requires it (for example, a valid order from a court or a government authority).

How long we keep it

Account and billing records are kept for as long as you are an Enercore customer, and afterwards for as long as Indian tax and company law requires us to retain invoices. Plant telemetry is kept for the life of the plant, because it is what your bills are calculated from. If your account is closed, we delete your personal profile data unless we are legally required to keep it.

Your rights

You can ask us to:

  • show you the personal data we hold about you,
  • correct anything that is wrong,
  • delete your account and personal data (subject to the legal retention above), or
  • stop using your data in a particular way.

Write to sales@enercore.org and we will respond within 30 days. You can also change your name, contact details and photo yourself from the Profile screen in the app.

Changes to this policy

If we change how we handle your data, we will update this page and change the date at the top. Material changes will also be communicated to you directly.
